Bus Classes

Business Class

(Business Class) - This class offers edges with wide and luxurious seats and salons. These edges are designed to have several chairs opposite a table in the middle. In addition, there is a salon at the end of the bus with 6 spacious, luxurious seats with a table in the middle. The flight on this class is non-stop, with a host on board offering free drinks and a free meal for customers.

Elite Plus Class

(Advanced Elite) - The edges of this class are characterized by the availability of Wi-Fi service and LCD screens in all passenger seats, as well as the availability of single seats. Gobas also offers a complimentary snack on this class for your entertainment throughout the journey. Jubas' rims travel everywhere in Egypt, offering fast, convenient and reliable booking methods, either by phone or the Internet, or through the application of Jubas to mobile phones.

Royal Plus

(Advanced Royal Class) - Our customers prefer this class for its distinctive and comfortable seats and its air-conditioned rims with a bathroom. The flight on this class is non-stop, which minimizes the time of the trip, as well as a host on board providing free and snack drinks to customers.

Classic Class

(Premium) - This class offers air-conditioned rims, a video player, luxurious seating with a complimentary meal and a host who provides drinks for free. The trip stops only once, which minimizes the time of the trip. Jubas' rims travel everywhere in Egypt, offering fast, convenient and reliable booking methods, both by phone, on the Internet, and through the application of Jubas to mobile phones.

De Luxe

(Grade A) - This class offers air-conditioned rims with a video player and comfortable seating. Jubas' rims travel everywhere in Egypt, offering fast, convenient and reliable booking methods, either by phone, the Internet, or through the application of Jubas to mobile phones.

Elite Class

(Elite class) - Racks of 31 passengers, equipped with double and single chairs to preserve the privacy of our customers. On board, a host provides free drinks and a meal to customers. The flight on this class is straight and non-stop, which minimizes flight time.
